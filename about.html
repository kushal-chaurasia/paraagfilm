WHO WE ARE?

Our company is new but our team is experienced. Our team is lead by Arvind yayawar. Since 1998 he is working as Column writer for news magazines, story writing in magazines, had done 20 TV serials for different DD stations, 12 short films, 10 documentaries, 4 DV full length hindi films, 1 full length ENGLISH film for Canadian producer Cris Reddey, 4 language full length film and more then 10 music albums. He has written and directed many ad films and corporate films. Along with these he was associated as episode writer and director for Rastriya Shaikshik Doordarshan (lucknow) from 2000 to 2002.
We believe entertainment is a limitless system of communication. Our goal is to harness the powerful truth of our collective experience, and use that source material to create world-class visual storytelling. We challenge our audiences to open their eyes and their hearts to the narratives of our diverse world that may be outside their general scope -- be it of race, gender, sexual orientation, social class, age and/or ability
