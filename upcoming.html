Hindi feature film

Shuruat(Tentative)

In the upcoming movie, follow the journey of a group of friends navigating the challenges of post-academy life. As they grapple with the harsh realities of the job market, the stark contrast in their fortunes puts their friendship to the test. While some soar to success with lucrative job offers, others find themselves trapped in the cycle of low-paying jobs that barely sustain them.

Despite their determination to improve their circumstances, the friends encounter resistance from their parents, who had envisioned a different future for them. Blaming their parents for pushing them into engineering against their true passions, the friends wrestle with feelings of resentment and regret.

As they struggle to break free from the constraints imposed upon them, the friends embark on a journey of self-discovery, seeking to redefine their paths and pursue their true passions. Along the way, they confront societal expectations, familial pressures, and personal insecurities, learning valuable lessons about resilience, friendship, and the pursuit of happiness.

Join them as they navigate the twists and turns of life, confronting their demons, chasing their dreams, and ultimately finding redemption in the bonds of friendship. Don't miss the compelling tale of courage, perseverance, and the unbreakable spirit of youth in the upcoming movie.

Writer and Director: Arvind Yayawar
